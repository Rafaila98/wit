ABOUT

Women In Tech University Edition is a community that aims at empowering and supporting young women in the technology sector.

Many young girls are intuitively hesitant to take up a career in technology and have trouble trusting their abilities to succeed in tech-related fields. As a result, female presence is extremely low in such professions. This absence is also noticeable in universities, where very few girls choose a major in technology and usually avoid such courses, even if they find them interesting.
